Fields of Dispute

Initial Setup

  • Each player: Meeple ×5, Money ×3, Fuel ×3, PolCap ×3
  • Place the four policy cards face‑up (Active) on the Policy Board.
  • Set counters: Price 2 | Public Support 5 | Concessions 0 | Round 1
  • Shuffle the red Event deck and leave it face‑down.

Round Sequence (6 Rounds)

  1. Event – Flip the top event card and resolve its text.
    If a card was reserved by Media last round, resolve it first.
  2. Place Workers – Players alternate placing one meeple at a time onto any open action slot until all five are placed.
  3. Resolve Actions (left → right)
    ActionCostEffect
    Blockade1 Fuel / meeple+1 Personal Support and +1 Public Support
    Lobby EU1 PolCapRoll d6; on 4‑6 succeed:
    • Active → flip to Delayed
    • Delayed → drag to Enacted
    Leave the acting meeple on the card as a control marker.
    Negotiate Aid1 MoneyPrice −1
    Media—+1 Personal Support; reserve the next event card face‑down for next round.
    Farm Ops1 Fuel + 1 MoneyGain 2 PolCap
  4. End Step
    • Return action meeples (meeple on policy cards stay as markers).
    • If Public Support ≥ 8: Concessions +1, then Public Support −8 (repeat if still ≥ 8).
    • Round +1; pass the start‑player marker.

Scoring after Round 6

  • Personal Support
  • Concessions × 2
  • Policies with your meeple in Delayed × 2
  • Policies with your meeple in Enacted × 3
  • Left‑over resources × 1
